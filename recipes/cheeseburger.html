Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a classic American dish consisting of a beef patty grilled or fried and served on a bun with cheese and various toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef(80/20 blend)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 4 slices of cheese (American or cheddar)
  • 4 burgers buns
  • lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Condiments(ketchup,mustard,mayonnaise)

Instructions

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties, making them slightly larger than the burger buns.
  3. Season the patties generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  4. Grill or cook the patties in a skillet for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Top each patty with a slice of cheese and allow it to melt.
  6. While the patties are cooking, prepare the toppings by slicing the lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  7. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in the oven.
  8. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patty with melted cheese on the bottom bun, then adding the lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments of your choice.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.

That's a basic cheeseburger recipe, but you can customize it by adding other toppings such as bacon, avocado, or pickles to make it your own. Enjoy!
