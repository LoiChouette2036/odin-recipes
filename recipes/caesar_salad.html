Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a popular salad that features crisp romaine lettuce, croutons, and a creamy dressing made with anchovies, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and lemon juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves thoroughly, then chop them into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl.
  2. Add the croutons and Parmesan cheese to the bowl.
  3. To make the dressing, combine the olive oil, garlic, anchovy fillets, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice in a small bowl. Whisk together until the ingredients are well combined.
  4. Pour the dressing over the lettuce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the Caesar salad immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and croutons if desired.

You can also add grilled chicken, shrimp, or salmon to make the salad more substantial, or use a different type of lettuce such as kale or arugula to change the flavor profile. Enjoy!
